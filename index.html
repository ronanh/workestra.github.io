What is it

Workestra is a native web orchestrator intended for extended collaboration, allowing you to create a distributed workflow that goes beyond your information system while keeping you own processes private.

Why

Your processes matter. You know how to get your job done. We handle the rest.

Who is it intended for

  • IT departments, especially for companies collaborating in extended enterprise.
  • Startups working extensively in the cloud
  • Open Source and web enthusiasts looking for collaboration

Benefits

  • Workestra provides a secure platform that integrates nicely in your system where you can plug your own homemade services, and benefits from services developed by the growing community.
  • Design a complete workflow on the internet, and mix private operations behind your firewall with collaboration
  • Client APIs are available for several languages, pick your favorite and get you first scheduled process running in hours, not weeks !
  • Secure file sharing, task scheduling, services API, monitoring, efficient applicative security
